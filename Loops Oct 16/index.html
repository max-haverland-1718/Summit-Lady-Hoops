The Summit Storm Mission:

"Our mission is to motivate, inspire and prepare young women to work hard every rep, every game, in the classroom, for their school, community, family, and for each other. Summit Storm Basketball is committed to promoting athletic competition and leadership at the highest level. We are committed to winning, teaching the fundamentals of basketball, and preparing young women for life beyond high school."
